Beagle Dog

The Beagle is a breed of hound that is primarily used for hunting purposes. Known for its keen sense of smell and tracking instinct, the Beagle is also popular as a companion dog due to its gentle and friendly temperament.

Beagle Trivia

  1. Size: Beagles are small to medium-sized dogs, typically weighing between 20-25 pounds.
  2. History: The breed was developed in England for rabbit hunting.
  3. Famous for: Beagles are known for their excellent sense of smell and tracking ability.
  4. Coat: They have a short, smooth coat that comes in a variety of colors, including tri-color, lemon, and red and white.
  5. Temperament: Beagles are friendly, curious, and great with children and other pets.
